Yam and Egg sauce

Yam with Egg Sauce is a delicious and nutritious meal. Yams are nutrient-dense tuber vegetables. They are a great source of fiber, potassium, manganese, copper, and antioxidants.

Ingredient Measurement
Yam tuber 4 slices
Egg 7
Tomatoes 10
Bell pepper 5
Maggi 1 Cube
Onions 1 bulb
Vegetable Oil 5 Teaspoons

Procedure

  • Slice yam into 2-inch size and peel the skin off. Rinse in clean water till water runs clear and no residue in water. Put yam in a pan or pot, add enough water to cover it, add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft. Test with a fork, if it goes through, then the yam should be soft enough. Drain water from yam if you do not want it to get too soft.
  • Break eggs into a shallow bowl, whisk till egg white and Egg yolk is well combined, add salt and whisk to combine then set it aside.
  • Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat, add Soya oil ( can use vegetable oil also) heat for about 2-4 minutes.
  • Add chopped pepper and onions ( this should sizzle ) And fry for about 4-5 minutes or till oil floats on the sauce, Add chopped tomatoes, add salt, seasoning, curry and thyme (spices).
  • Stir to combine ingredients with sauce and also avoid sauce sticking to the pan.
  • Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute before stirring. ( this will allow the egg to set a bit) Stir to scramble egg.
  • Serve egg sauce with boiled yam immediately.
